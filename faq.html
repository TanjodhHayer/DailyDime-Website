Project Overview

Managing personal finances can be a daunting task for many individuals. The DailyDime362Proj team has developed a simple yet effective solution to address this challenge. Our app delves deep into tracking both spending and earnings, offering users a comprehensive understanding of their financial activities.

Project Pitch

  • We empower users by providing a seamless experience for adding incomes and expenses, ensuring that recording financial transactions is a straightforward and efficient process.
  • With daily summaries, users gain immediate insights into their total expenditures and earnings, highlighting the most common expense and income categories.
  • Our integrated search functionality leverages Google Maps and Places API, allowing users to conveniently access information about nearby banks. Additionally, users can enhance their investment portfolio by adding potential stocks through our integration with Alpha Vantage.
  • Using a calendar interface, our app elegantly showcases the total amounts of money spent or earned on specific days, providing a comprehensive overview of financial activities over time.

Theme

Our app boasts a sleek and luxurious design with a color scheme inspired by this Dribble shot. The combination of black, grey, and white elements creates a cool and sophisticated user interface.
